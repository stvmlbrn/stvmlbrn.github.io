About Me

Hi, I'm Steve. I like to write code.

I started out in the world of technology in the late 1990s as a computer technician and network support engineer. I enjoyed the work, and for the most part still do whenever it comes up, but I quickly began to realize that software is what makes the world go around. So, I started studying programming and began writing code. The early days were desktop applications written mostly in Visual Basic before moving on to the web with the early versions of ColdFusion.

After realizing I wanted to write software for a living, somewhere around 2002, I decided to go back to school and obtain a Master's degree in Computer Science. At that time I was a 100% self-taught developer and felt that a formal education would help me in some areas where my self-education may be lacking. I completed the Master's degree in 2007.

These days I'm focusing on JavaScript platforms with Node.js and React.js but I'm always willing and eager to learn new stacks. I'll never be mistaken as a designer but I do enjoy working on all layers of the web application stack including front-end code, server APIs, database design/development, server administration, and so on.

If you or your team needs some help or are looking for a new team member and think I may be a good fit, I'd love to talk about it.
