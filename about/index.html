About Me

Hi, I'm Steve. I like to write code.

I started out in the world of technology in the late 1990s as a computer technician and network support engineer. I enjoyed the work, and for the most part still do whenever it comes up, but I quickly began to realize that software is what makes the world go around. So, I started developing desktop applications with Visual Basic before moving to the web and began using ColdFusion. It was then I realized that developing web applications is what I enjoy the most.

After realizing I wanted to write software for a living, somewhere around 2002, I decided to go back to school and obtain a Master's degree in Computer Science (I already had Bachelor degrees in History and Philosophy. The plan was to be a teacher - but fate had other plans!). At that time I was a 100% self-taught developer and felt that a formal education would help me in some areas where my self-education may be lacking. I completed my Master's degree in 2007.

I'm currently focusing on JavaScript platforms with Node.js and React.js. I'll never be mistaken as a designer but I do enjoy working on all areas of the stack, including front-end code, server APIs, database design/development, server administration, and so on. If you or your team needs some help and think I may be a good fit, I'm certainly interested in talking about it.

I currently live in Cumberland, MD with my wife and 3 sons.
