About Me

Hi, I'm Steve. I like to write code.

I started out in technology in the late 1990s as a computer technician and network support engineer. I enjoyed the work, and for the most part still do whenever it comes up, but I soon realized that I wanted to get into software development. So I started studying programming and began writing code. The early days were desktop applications written mostly in Visual Basic before moving on to the web with the early versions of ColdFusion.

After realizing I wanted to write software for a living I decided to go back to school to study computer science. At that time I was a 100% self-taught developer and felt that a formal education would help me in some areas where my self-education may be lacking. I completed a Master's degree computer science in 2007.

These days I'm focusing on JavaScript platforms with Node.js and React but I'm always willing and eager to learn new stacks. I love web development and enjoy working on all layers of the web application stack. I'm looking for new and interesting work and hope to join a team of talented engineers and do cool stuff. If you or your team needs some help or are looking for a new team member and think I may be a good fit, I'd love to talk about it.

If you happen to be in the western Maryland area you should come to our develper meetups! We are a small group of developers from all backgrounds that get together and geek out about programming and tech. Check us out @QueenCityHacks.
